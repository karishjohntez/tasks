short story

Immediately after independence, there was a great thirst of education and especially Secondary and University education. Sakwa High School was started with that requirement in mind. It opened its doors on 29th March 1965 with 71 students. These students started learning in a house which belonged to a white settler just below Ruty Shopping Centre near the main road. Sakwa High School currently occupies a land of 54 acres.

Our Goals

"Sakwa High School aims to prepare students to make a positive contribution to the development of the society and to acquire the values of national patriotism, self-respect, self-reliance, cooperation, adaptability, a sense of purpose and self-discipline. "

Our Objectives

School's Motto:Striving for excellence. School's Mission: To produce men and women of integrity to serve this nation. School's Vision: To make Sakwa High School a Centre of academic excellence in the country.
